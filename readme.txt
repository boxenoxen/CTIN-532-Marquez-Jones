This project uses the W,A,S,D & E Keys, as well as the up, down, right, and left arrows and "right shift.  
Subtitles objects, boulder spawner, and parallaxing tool were imported from Asset Store.
Original sprites were downloaded from Google Images and edited in Adobe Photoshop.
Honk, kiss, and whistle sounds were downloaded from freesounds.org.
All music is original.
Metrics script was edited and written by Jung-Ho Sohn for class.
